about=While active, Directory Sync watches a chosen folder (recursively) using a live filesystem watcher. New spatial files are loaded as layers, removed files have their layers removed, and edited files are reloaded in place while preserving styling and layer-tree position. Supports any GDAL/OGR-readable format, including multi-layer GeoPackages, and applies sidecar .qml styles automatically.
